# OSINT Email Verification Tool
Python based OSINT tool for email verification and analysis

## Overview
The OSINT Email Verification Tool is a Python-based Open Source Intelligence (OSINT) utility designed to help individuals and businesses analyse email addresses for legitimacy, risk, and exposure.

It is built to support cybersecurity investigations, fraud prevention, due diligence, and general digital trust assessment.

---

## Problem Statement
Fake, disposable, and compromised email addresses are commonly used in:
- Phishing attacks
- Online fraud
- Account takeovers
- Fake registrations
- Social engineering campaigns

Manually investigating an email address is time-consuming and error-prone.

---

## Solution
This tool automates email intelligence gathering by analysing:
- Email format validity
- Domain existence and DNS records
- Public exposure indicators
- Risk signals commonly used in OSINT investigations

The goal is to provide fast, structured insight to support informed decisions.

---

## Features
- Email syntax validation
- Domain and DNS checks
- OSINT-friendly analysis output
- Designed for automation and future expansion
- Lightweight and beginner-friendly
